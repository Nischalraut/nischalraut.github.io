Philosophy & Approach

Engineering is not merely about solving equations; it is about managing the forces of nature to achieve elegance in motion. My approach combines rigorous mathematical analysis with hands-on experimentation. Whether it's validating a CFD model in a wind tunnel or integrating complex avionics, I thrive in the iterative cycle of Design, Test, and Analyze.

My journey began with a curiosity about how heavy metal objects could defy gravity, which evolved into a specialized focus on Aerodynamics and System Integration.

Beyond Engineering

When I'm not running simulations or soldering flight controllers, I am deeply immersed in the world of RC Aviation. Building and flying my own aircraft gives me an intuitive understanding of flight mechanics that textbooks cannot provide.
