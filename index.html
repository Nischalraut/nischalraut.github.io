Subsonic Wind Tunnel

Problem

Lack of experimental facilities to study gust effects on airfoils.

Method

Designed an Open-Jet Wind Tunnel with an active Gust Generator. Conducted flow quality assessment using pitot-static probes and hot-wire anemometry.

Result

Achieved a Turbulent Intensity of 0.88% in the test section. Successfully validated gust profiles against theoretical models.

B-52 Air Launch Carrier

Scope: Conceptual Design & Performance Analysis

This project explored the feasibility of retrofitting the B-52 Stratofortress to launch small satellites.

Using historical data and flight envelope calculations, we determined the optimal release altitude and velocity to maximize payload capacity for Low Earth Orbit (LEO) insertion. The study concluded that air-launch offers significant delta-V savings compared to ground launch.
